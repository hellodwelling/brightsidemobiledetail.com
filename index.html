Our Services

What we are best at - expert automotive detailing

Wash & Wax

Just that! Drive through the mud lately?

Exterior Detail

Washing of all exterior surfaces and wheel wells. Removal of surface contaminant, surface scratches, water spots, swirl marks. Polishing and wax protective coating. Deep cleaning, shining, and protective coating of rims, tires, and wheels.

Interior Detail

Deep cleaning of all vents, cracks, and crevices. Vacuuming and shampooing of floors, mats, headliners, and seats. Cleaning of all Interior surfaces, doors, and windows. Finished off with a layer of shine and protectant.

Complete Detail

This detail package comes with both an Interior and Exterior detail.

Headlight Restoration

Cloudy or yellowed headlights? Improper restoration can leave them in the same condition after a few weeks. Let us do it the right way for you.

Ceramic Coating

Ceramic coating is essential to fill the porous surface of your paint or gel coat, that will prevent small pieces of dirt to enter the painted surface.
